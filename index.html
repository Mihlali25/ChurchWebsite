About Our Church

Holiness Union Church Cape Town was founded in 2021 with a vision to spread the gospel and serve our community. We are a Bible-believing, Christ-centered church committed to spiritual growth and community transformation.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, discipleship, and service. We welcome people from all walks of life to experience God's love and grace.

At Holiness Union Church, we believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the importance of Christian fellowship. Join us as we walk together in God's grace.
